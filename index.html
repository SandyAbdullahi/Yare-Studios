Who We Are

Yare Picture Studios was founded in 2015 in Nairobi with a vision to create impactful and visually stunning narratives that engage audiences. Since its founding, the company has established itself as a leader in the film production industry, earning recognition for its unique and cinematic approach to visual storytelling. Over the years, Yare has built a reputation for delivering high-quality productions across a wide range of genres, from commercial to documentary.

Yare Picture Studios continues to push the boundaries of what is possible in the world of film. Despite its growth and success, the company remains true to its roots, always prioritizing the creative process and the collaborative relationship with its clients.
