Behind the Lens

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Yare Picture Studios began as a tight‑knit collective of filmmakers united by a passion for bold visual storytelling. From humble beginnings shooting passion projects on Nairobi’s bustling streets, we’ve grown into a full‑service production house delivering award‑winning work across Africa and beyond.

Our ethos is simple: Craft every frame with purpose. Whether documenting real‑life heroes or building cinematic worlds from scratch, we balance technical mastery with authentic emotion—because stories resonate when they feel lived‑in, honest, and fearless.

Today, our slate spans feature documentaries, narrative films, brand campaigns, and end‑to‑end production solutions. But we’re still that same spirited crew chasing the next great shot—only now with bigger toys and louder applause.

Meet the Team

Abdi Shuria

Founder | Director | Filmmaker

Certifications: Kenya Film Classifications Board (KFCB)

Abdi Shuria is an award-winning Kenyan filmmaker, cinematographer and executive producer. As founder of Yare Picture Studio he helms some of East Africa’s most talked-about screen stories, including the Showmax originals Black & Blue (2024), Faithless (2023) and Igiza (2022). A graduate of the Academy of Art University (San Francisco), Abdi’s dynamic visual style was honed on the multi-award-winning satirical series The XYZ Show, which he directed through Seasons 12 & 13. He continues to champion bold African narratives while mentoring the next generation of East African screen talent.
